# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project should be a practical and respectful place to collaborate. Be
clear, patient, and constructive. Assume good intent, but take responsibility
for the impact of your words and changes.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Giving actionable feedback.
- Explaining tradeoffs without personal attacks.
- Keeping discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Being respectful of different experience levels.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Using the project spaces to spam or promote unrelated work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that do not meet this standard. Repeated or severe violations may
result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the repository maintainer
privately through GitHub.
